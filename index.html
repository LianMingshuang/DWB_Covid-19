Project Background
Our project team consists of Jiang He, Rowan Moody, Qihui Wang, and Smruti Naik. The four of us created this sample webpage and
correspoonding products for our client, Doctors Without Borders (MSF), as part of the IDCE30262 Webmapping course at Clark University.

Project Purpose
Our project is designed to facilitate decision making, increased public access to resources, and elevaed awareness of MSF project efforts.
It is centered around outbreaks of COVID-19 virus in Massachusetts, since this is a global issue at this time and an important focus for the MSF.

Caveat: This is a theoretical project showing the capabilities of web mapping tools. We are not affiliated with MSF.
