Product 1 Walkthough
To explore this product, toggle layers and click in-map for town level statistics. The COVID-19 data is displayed in a sliding layer
for ease of comparison. To access the individual feature layers displayed in the map, click 'More information' in the About tab to access
the hosted ArcGIS Online page. Share this webmap link or embed code by clicking into the Share menu on the top-right corner of the webmap.
